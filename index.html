These Smart Money Tools are five powerful, easy-to-use calculators that gather the most important numbers for your financial life. From budgeting and saving to debt, retirement, and big-purchase planning, these tools help you collect the critical figures you need to make better decisions. They’re designed to be simple and intuitive—no math expertise required—so you can focus on your goals, not the formulas.

TVM Financial Calculator This works just like a pocket financial calculator. In addition to arithmetic, it can also calculate present value, future value, payments or number of periods.
Savings Goal CalculatorWhat will it take to reach your savings goal? This financial calculator helps you find out.
Loan CalculatorTake a close look at your loan, including how much you could save by paying it off early.
Retirement Progress CalculatorHow much will you need to retire? Is your current plan taking you on the right path? Use this calculator to find out.
Retirement Account Tax CalculatorHow much difference does using a tax-deferred (401(k), IRA, etc) or a tax free (Roth 401(k), Roth IRA, etc) account make to your retirement savings.
